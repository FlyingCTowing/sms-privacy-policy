SMS Communication Policy

Effective Date: 11/23/2024

At Flying C Towing, LLC, we respect your privacy and are committed to using your personal information responsibly. This SMS Communication Policy outlines how we collect, use, and manage your information when you communicate with us via SMS. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

Collection of Phone Numbers

We collect your phone number and name only when you voluntarily provide them by contacting us via SMS, such as when you reach out for quotes or services. By initiating communication with us via SMS, you consent to receive replies and updates related to your inquiries or requested services.

Use of Phone Numbers for SMS

Your phone number is used exclusively to communicate with you regarding:

  • Quotes for towing and related services
  • Updates about your service request
  • Information about our offerings

We do not use your phone number for promotional or marketing purposes. Your information will not be shared with third parties or used beyond the scope of providing service updates or responding to your inquiries.

Opting Out of SMS Communications

If at any point you no longer wish to receive SMS messages from Flying C Towing, LLC, you can opt out by texting any of the following keywords to our SMS number:

  • STOP
  • UNSUBSCRIBE
  • CANCEL
  • QUIT

Once you opt out, we will cease all SMS communications. Please allow up to 10 business days for your request to be fully processed.

Privacy of Your Information

Flying C Towing, LLC values your privacy. We will never sell, transfer, or share your phone number or name with any third party. All information collected is securely maintained and used solely for the purpose of facilitating communication with you.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. If significant updates are made, we will notify you by posting a revised version on our website.
